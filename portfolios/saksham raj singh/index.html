Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my services, process, and more.

What software do you use for editing and color grading?

+

I primarily use industry-standard software like DaVinci Resolve Studio for advanced color grading and Adobe Premiere Pro for comprehensive video editing to ensure the highest quality output.

What is your typical turnaround time for a project?

+

Turnaround time varies depending on the complexity and length of the project. A typical project can range from 3-7 business days. We'll establish a clear timeline during our initial consultation.

How many revisions are included in your service?

+

My standard packages typically include 2-3 rounds of revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product. Additional revisions can be accommodated at an agreed-upon rate.

What kind of content do you specialize in editing?

+

I specialize in creating high-quality, engaging video edits with a dark & premium aesthetic. This includes content for YouTube, vlogs, documentaries, promotional videos, short films, and social media content.

How do I provide my raw footage to you?

+

I accept footage via secure cloud storage platforms like Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer. We'll discuss the best method for your specific project during our onboarding.
