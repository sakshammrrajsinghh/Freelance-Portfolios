Frequently Asked Questions

What types of videos do you specialize in?

I specialize in high-quality, engaging video edits for content creators and YouTubers across various niches, including vlogs, tutorials, product reviews, short films, and promotional content. My focus is on delivering a premium, cinematic feel.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Turnaround time varies based on the complexity and length of the project. For most standard YouTube videos (10-20 minutes), I aim for 3-5 business days. We'll discuss and agree on a clear deadline during the briefing phase.

How do you handle revisions?

My packages typically include 2-3 rounds of revisions to ensure you are completely satisfied with the final product. Additional revisions can be accommodated at an agreed-upon rate.

What software do you use?

I primarily use Adobe Premiere Pro for editing and DaVinci Resolve for advanced color grading, ensuring industry-standard quality and flexibility.
