My Creative Process

A step-by-step guide to how we'll bring your video vision to life.

1. Consultation & Concept

We start with a detailed discussion about your project, goals, target audience, and desired aesthetic. Sharing references is highly encouraged!

2. Footage Submission & Organization

You provide your raw footage and any assets (music, graphics). I'll organize and prepare everything for an efficient editing workflow.

3. Editing & First Draft

I dive into the edit, focusing on storytelling, pacing, transitions, and initial color correction to create a compelling first draft for your review.

4. Feedback & Revisions

You review the draft and provide feedback. I then make necessary revisions to align perfectly with your vision and expectations.

5. Final Color Grade & Delivery

Once the edit is approved, I apply the final, cinematic color grade and deliver the high-quality video in your preferred format, ready for publishing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? I've got answers to help you get started.

What kind of projects do you specialize in? +
I specialize in high-quality, engaging video edits and professional color grading for content creators, YouTubers, vloggers, and anyone needing a premium, modern dark aesthetic for their video content.
What's your typical turnaround time? +
Turnaround time depends on the project's complexity and length. Once we discuss your specific needs, I'll provide an estimated timeline. Rush services may be available for an additional fee.
How do you handle revisions? +
Each project includes a set number of revisions (usually 2-3 rounds) to ensure you're completely satisfied. We'll clarify this during our initial consultation. Additional revisions can be accommodated.
What software do you use? +
I primarily use industry-standard software like Adobe Premiere Pro for editing and DaVinci Resolve for advanced color grading, ensuring professional and compatible deliverables.
How do we communicate during the project? +
I maintain clear and consistent communication via email, video calls, or a project management tool, whichever is most convenient for you. Regular updates are a standard part of my process.
