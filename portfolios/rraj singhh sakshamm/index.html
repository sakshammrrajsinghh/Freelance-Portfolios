About Me

Hello! I'm Rraj Singhh Sakshamm, a dedicated full-time freelancer passionate about bringing stories to life through exceptional video. With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to quality, I specialize in transforming raw footage into captivating visual experiences.

My expertise lies in both Video Editing and Color Grading. I thrive on collaborating with content creators, YouTubers, and businesses who demand nothing less than high-quality, engaging videos that truly resonate with their audience.

I understand the nuances of storytelling, pacing, and visual aesthetics, ensuring every project I undertake not only meets but exceeds expectations. Let's create something extraordinary together.

My Expertise

Video Editing

Crafting seamless narratives, dynamic transitions, and engaging pacing to keep your audience hooked from start to finish. From documentaries to vlogs, I handle all genres with precision.

Color Grading

Transforming the mood and atmosphere of your footage with professional color correction and grading. I ensure your visuals are vibrant, consistent, and cinematic, matching your brand's aesthetic.

Sound Design & Mixing

Enhancing your video's impact with crisp audio, compelling sound effects, and perfectly mixed music. A great video deserves equally great sound to truly immerse the viewer.

Motion Graphics & VFX

Adding a touch of magic with custom intros, lower thirds, animated text, and subtle visual effects to elevate your production value and convey information creatively.

Frequently Asked Questions

What software do you use for editing and color grading?

I primarily work with Adobe Premiere Pro for editing and DaVinci Resolve for advanced color grading, ensuring industry-standard quality and flexibility.

How do you handle revisions?

My process includes a set number of revision rounds (usually 2-3, depending on the project scope) to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product. Clear communication is key!

What kind of turnaround time can I expect?

Turnaround time varies greatly based on the complexity and length of the project. We'll discuss and agree upon a realistic deadline during our initial consultation, and I always strive to deliver efficiently.

Do you offer custom packages for long-term collaborations?

Absolutely! I'm always looking for long-term partnerships with content creators and YouTubers. We can discuss retainer models or custom packages tailored to your ongoing needs. Contact me to explore options!
