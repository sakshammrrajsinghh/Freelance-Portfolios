Frequently Asked Questions

What types of videos do you specialize in?

+

I specialize in a wide range of content, including YouTube videos, vlogs, short films, promotional content, corporate videos, and social media clips. My focus is on creating high-quality, engaging visual stories for content creators and brands.

What's your typical turnaround time?

+

Turnaround time varies depending on the complexity and length of the project. For most standard projects, it's typically 3-7 business days from receiving all necessary assets. We'll agree on a clear timeline during our initial consultation.

How do you handle revisions?

+

My packages typically include a set number of revision rounds (usually 2-3) to ensure you are completely satisfied with the final product. Additional revisions can be accommodated, often at an hourly rate.

What software do you use?

+

I primarily use industry-standard software like Adobe Premiere Pro for editing and DaVinci Resolve for advanced color grading, ensuring professional results and compatibility.
