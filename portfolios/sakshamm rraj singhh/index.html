Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of videos do you specialize in? ▼
I specialize in video editing and color grading for a wide range of content, including YouTube videos, promotional content, vlogs, short films, social media clips, and more. My focus is on creating engaging visuals for content creators and brands.
How long does a typical project take? ▼
Project timelines vary significantly based on the length of the raw footage, desired complexity of the edit, inclusion of motion graphics, and number of revisions. After our initial consultation, I'll provide a clear estimated timeline for your specific project.
What software do you use? ▼
I primarily use industry-standard software such as Adobe Premiere Pro for editing and DaVinci Resolve for advanced color grading, ensuring professional results and compatibility.
What is your revision policy? ▼
Typically, 2-3 rounds of revisions are included in the project cost to ensure you are completely satisfied with the final product. Any additional revisions can be discussed and arranged.
How do we get started? ▼
Simply click the "Contact Me" button or send me an email! We'll schedule a brief chat to discuss your project needs, scope, and how I can help bring your vision to life.
