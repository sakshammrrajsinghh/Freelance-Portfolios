Frequently Asked Questions

What software do you use for editing and color grading? ▼

I primarily use industry-standard software such as Adobe Premiere Pro for editing, DaVinci Resolve for advanced color grading, and Adobe After Effects for motion graphics and visual effects.

What is your typical turnaround time for a project? ▼

Turnaround time varies depending on the complexity and length of the project. For most standard videos (up to 10-15 minutes), it typically ranges from 3 to 7 business days. We'll establish a clear timeline during our discovery call.

How do you handle revisions? ▼

My packages typically include 2-3 rounds of revisions to ensure you are completely satisfied with the final product. Additional revisions can be accommodated at an agreed-upon rate.

Do you offer custom packages for specific needs? ▼

Absolutely! While I have standard service offerings, I'm happy to create a custom package tailored specifically to your project's unique requirements and budget. Let's discuss your needs.

How do I send you my footage and files? ▼

You can easily share your raw footage and other assets via cloud storage services like Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer. I'll provide detailed instructions on the best method for your project.
