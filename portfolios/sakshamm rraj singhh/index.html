Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of videos do you specialize in editing? +

I specialize in editing a wide range of content, primarily for content creators and YouTubers. This includes vlogs, tutorials, product reviews, short documentaries, promotional content, and more. My focus is on creating engaging, high-quality visuals.

What is your typical turnaround time? +

Turnaround time varies depending on the complexity and length of the project. For most standard YouTube videos (10-15 mins raw footage), it's typically 3-5 business days. We'll discuss and agree on a precise timeline during our initial consultation.

How do you handle revisions? +

My packages typically include 1-2 rounds of revisions to ensure you are completely satisfied with the final product. Additional revisions can be accommodated at an agreed-upon rate.

What software do you use for editing and color grading? +

I primarily use Adobe Premiere Pro for video editing and DaVinci Resolve for advanced color grading, ensuring industry-standard quality and flexibility.

How do we get started on a project? +

Simply click the "Contact Me" button below or send me an email. We'll schedule a quick chat to discuss your project, and if it's a good fit, I'll send you a proposal!
