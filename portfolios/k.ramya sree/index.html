Frequently Asked Questions

What is your turnaround time?

Turnaround time varies based on the complexity and length of the project. Typically, smaller projects can be completed within 3-5 business days, while larger projects may take 1-2 weeks. We'll establish a clear timeline during our initial consultation.

What software do you use for editing and color grading?

I primarily use Adobe Premiere Pro for editing and DaVinci Resolve for professional color grading, ensuring industry-standard quality and flexibility.

Can you work with any type of raw footage?

Yes, I can work with a wide range of camera formats and codecs. Whether it's DSLR, mirrorless, cinema camera, or even smartphone footage, I can process it efficiently.

Do you offer revisions?

Absolutely! My standard package includes 2 rounds of revisions to ensure you are 100% satisfied with the final product. Additional revisions can be arranged if needed.

How do you handle large file transfers?

I typically use secure cloud storage solutions like Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer for efficient and safe transfer of large video files.
