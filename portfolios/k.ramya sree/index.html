My Workflow Process

A clear, step-by-step approach to bring your video vision to life.

1

Consultation & Planning

We start with a detailed discussion of your project goals, vision, and specific requirements. We'll outline timelines, deliverables, and expectations to ensure alignment.

2

Footage Organization & Rough Cut

Upon receiving your footage, I'll organize and sync it, then create a rough cut to establish the foundational narrative and pacing of your video.

3

Fine Editing & Sound Design

This stage involves refining cuts, adding transitions, incorporating graphics, and crafting professional sound design (music, SFX, voiceover clean-up).

4

Color Grading & Finishing

The video gets its final cinematic look with expert color correction and grading. I then add any final touches and prepare it for review.

5

Review & Delivery

You'll receive a preview for feedback and revisions. Once approved, I'll deliver the final high-quality video in your preferred formats, ready for publication.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my services and process.

What kind of videos do you specialize in? +

I specialize in a wide range of content, including YouTube videos, vlogs, corporate explainers, short films, social media content, and promotional videos. My focus is on delivering high-quality, engaging edits with cinematic color grading across all genres.

What is your typical turnaround time? +

Turnaround time varies depending on the complexity and length of the project. For most standard projects, it's typically 3-7 business days. We will discuss and agree upon a clear timeline during our initial consultation.

What software do you use for editing and color grading? +

I primarily use industry-standard software such as Adobe Premiere Pro for editing and DaVinci Resolve for advanced color grading. This ensures professional-grade results and compatibility.

How do we share footage and communicate? +

For footage transfer, I recommend secure cloud services like Google Drive (which you already use), Dropbox, or WeTransfer. Communication will be via email, scheduled video calls, or a project management tool as preferred.

Do you offer revisions? +

Yes, I typically include 2-3 rounds of revisions to ensure you are completely satisfied with the final product. Additional revisions can be accommodated at an agreed-upon rate.
