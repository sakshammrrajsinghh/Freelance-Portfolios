Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical turnaround time? +

Turnaround time varies depending on the project's complexity and length. For standard projects, it's typically 3-7 business days. We'll discuss a precise timeline during our initial consultation.

What kind of footage do you work with? +

I work with various formats and resolutions, from DSLR/mirrorless footage to cinematic raw files. As long as the footage quality is decent, I can make it shine.

How many revisions are included? +

My packages typically include 2-3 rounds of revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product. Additional revisions can be arranged if needed.

Do you offer custom packages? +

Absolutely! Every project is unique. Contact me with your specific needs, and I'll create a tailored package and quote that fits your requirements and budget.
