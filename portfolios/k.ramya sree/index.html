Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? I've got answers. Find common inquiries about my services and process here.

How do you determine pricing for projects? +
Pricing is typically based on the complexity, duration of the raw footage, desired output length, and specific services required (e.g., advanced VFX, sound design). I offer custom quotes after a detailed discussion of your project.
What is your typical turnaround time? +
Turnaround time varies greatly depending on the project's scope. Small projects might be a few days, while larger ones can take weeks. We'll discuss and agree upon a realistic timeline during the initial brief.
What kind of raw footage do you need? +
I can work with various formats from professional cameras (DSLRs, mirrorless, cinema cameras) to high-quality smartphone footage. Providing organized footage and a clear brief helps streamline the process.
Can you match a specific style or aesthetic? +
Absolutely! While my preferred style is dark & premium, I'm highly adaptable. Providing reference videos or examples of what you like will help me tailor the edit to your desired aesthetic.
What's included in 'Color Grading'? +
Color grading includes color correction (balancing exposure, white balance, contrast), and creative grading (applying a specific look or mood to enhance storytelling and visual appeal).
