My Workflow

1

Discovery & Planning

We start with a detailed discussion about your project, goals, and vision. I gather all necessary footage and assets to understand your needs completely.

2

Editing & First Draft

I meticulously edit your footage, focusing on pacing, flow, and narrative. A first draft is then delivered for your review and initial feedback.

3

Revisions & Refinement

Based on your feedback, I make necessary adjustments and refine the edit. This iterative process ensures the video aligns perfectly with your expectations.

4

Color Grading & Final Polish

The video undergoes professional color grading to achieve the desired look and feel. Final audio mix, visual effects, and quality checks are performed.

5

Delivery

The high-quality, final video is delivered to you in your preferred format, ready to captivate your audience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of videos do you specialize in editing? +

I specialize in a wide range of content, including YouTube videos, vlogs, documentaries, short-form content (Reels, TikToks), promotional videos, and educational content. My goal is always to create engaging and high-quality visuals tailored to your specific needs.

What is your typical turnaround time? +

Turnaround time varies depending on the project's complexity and length. For most standard projects, I aim for 3-7 business days for the first draft. We'll discuss a precise timeline during our initial consultation.

How do you handle revisions? +

I typically include 2-3 rounds of revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product. Your feedback is crucial, and I'm committed to making sure the video meets your vision.

What software do you use? +

I primarily use Adobe Premiere Pro for editing and DaVinci Resolve for advanced color grading, ensuring industry-standard quality and flexibility for various project types.
